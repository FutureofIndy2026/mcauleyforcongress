Policy Priorities

Trump-Aligned, Indiana-First Agenda

Patrick stands with President Trump on an America First vision: secure borders, strong police, energy independence, and an economy that works for working families in Indianapolis, not just Washington insiders.

America-First Economy

Jobs, manufacturing, and fair trade for American workers.

  • Support fair trade policies that put U.S. workers first.
  • Push back on China’s unfair economic practices.
  • Cut red tape that crushes small businesses and job creators.

A stronger national economy means more opportunity in Indy.

Border Security & Immigration

Secure the border, end chaos, and stop fentanyl.

  • Finish and reinforce the southern border wall.
  • End catch-and-release and enforce existing immigration law.
  • Crack down on cartels and fentanyl flooding into our communities.

Border security is a public safety issue for every city.

Law & Order

Back the blue and restore safety to our neighborhoods.

  • Support police recruitment, training, and retention.
  • Strengthen community policing and neighborhood partnerships.
  • Hold violent repeat offenders accountable with tougher penalties.

Safe streets are the foundation for strong neighborhoods.

Energy Independence

Lower prices by making America energy dominant again.

  • Expand responsible U.S. oil and natural gas production.
  • Support an all-of-the-above strategy, including nuclear.
  • Cut regulations that drive up gas and utility bills for families.

Hoosier families deserve affordable, reliable energy.

Lower Taxes & Cost of Living

Let families and small businesses keep more of what they earn.

  • Protect and strengthen the Trump tax cuts.
  • Fight inflationary spending and waste in Washington.
  • Oppose IRS overreach into the lives of ordinary taxpayers.

Washington should live within its means—just like families do.

Business & Workforce Growth

Grow jobs through small business and skilled trades.

  • Cut red tape that blocks new housing and business growth.
  • Expand apprenticeships and trade pathways for young people.
  • Partner with Indiana employers to connect students to careers.

Real-world skills, not bureaucracy, drive prosperity.

Parental Rights & Education

Parents first. Schools that teach, not indoctrinate.

  • Support school choice and competition in education.
  • Protect parents’ rights to know what their kids are being taught.
  • Strengthen career and technical education options.

Classrooms should focus on reading, writing, math, and civics.

Constitutional Freedoms

Defend the Second Amendment and religious liberty.

  • Protect lawful gun owners and oppose federal registries.
  • Defend religious liberty at home, work, and in public life.
  • Stand up for free speech and against government censorship.

Our rights come from God, not from government.

Indianapolis Focus

Putting Indianapolis First

Patrick’s America First approach is grounded in his experience in Indianapolis real estate, business, and neighborhood-level politics. This isn’t theory—it’s about real results for families in Marion County and Central Indiana.

Local priorities include:
• Expanding workforce housing near major job centers.
• Supporting law enforcement and community policing partnerships.
• Partnering with employers to grow apprenticeships and skilled trades.
• Cutting red tape that blocks common-sense development and job growth.
